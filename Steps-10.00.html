Step 2: Transform Data in Power Query Editor

1)    On Home Ribbon, click on Transform Data

2)    Click Remove Time Column

3)    Location  and Flight # columns has a typo, there are incorrect locations and we need to fix the mistakes. You must select the misspelled version and replace the value with the correct spelling. In order to do this, you must select the "Location" tab and replace it. 

-      Location:

·         Change the following: "Off Turks and Caicos Islands” =“Santiago, Dominican Republic"

·         “Off Sabine Pass, Texas" = "Sabine Pass, Texas"

·         "Off Lihue, Hawaii"=   "Lihue, Hawaii"

4)    Click on Close and Apply
